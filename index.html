About

I'm a Senior Technical Program Manager at DoorDash with over six years of experience shipping products that operate at the intersection of fintech, logistics, and marketplace infrastructure.

With a dual graduate background in computer science and finance, I bring a rare combination of technical depth and business acumen to every initiative. I've led programs spanning payments, compliance, routing optimization, and platform architecture — consistently delivering measurable outcomes across 10+ cross-functional teams.

I thrive in high-ambiguity environments where the path forward isn't clear. My approach: define the problem sharply, align stakeholders early, and drive relentlessly toward impact.
